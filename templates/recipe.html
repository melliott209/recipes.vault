Ham Sandwich

A simple ham sandwich with mayo.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 slice ham
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Spread mayonnaise on bread.
  2. Place ham on one slice of bread.
  3. Close the sandwich with the other slice of bread.
